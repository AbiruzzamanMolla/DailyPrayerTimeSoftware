# Privacy Policy for Daily Prayer Timer

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Daily Prayer Timer ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Daily Prayer Timer application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Location Data
To provide accurate prayer times, the application requires your geographical coordinates (Latitude and Longitude). This data is provided by you manually or through your system's location services.

### 1.2 User Settings
The application stores your preferences (such as calculation methods, school of thought, and notification settings) to provide a customized experience.

## 2. How Your Information is Used

### 2.1 Local Processing
By default, location data and settings are stored locally on your device in the application's data folder. 

### 2.2 External API Services
If you enable the "External API" setting, your latitude, longitude, and calculation method are sent to the [AlAdhan API](https://aladhan.com/prayer-times-api) to retrieve prayer times. No personally identifiable information (PII) is sent to this service.

## 3. Data Storage
All application data (cached prayer times, settings, and logs) is stored locally on your machine. We do not maintain any central database of user information.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The application may use the following third-party services:
- **AlAdhan API**: Used to fetch prayer times and Hijri dates (if enabled).
- **GitHub**: Used for hosting source code, releases, and optional update checks.

## 5. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the integrity of the application code. However, as the software is open-source and runs locally, you are responsible for securing your own device.

## 6. Your Rights
Since no personal data is stored on our servers, you can "delete" all your data by simply uninstalling the application and removing its data folder from your `%AppData%` directory.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our GitHub repository.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our [GitHub Issues page](https://github.com/AbiruzzamanMolla/DailyPrayerTimeSoftware/issues).
