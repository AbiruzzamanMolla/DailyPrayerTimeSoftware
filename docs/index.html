Capabilities

Engineered with purpose.

Native Integration

Dual Taskbar Modes including Windows 11 TrafficMonitor-style integration and legacy DeskBand. Lives in your system tray, always ready.

Zen Mode

Immersive focus modes that strip away distractions. Just the prayer countdown — pure, simple, beautiful.

Jamaat Sync

Track fixed Jamaat times for your local Masjid. Time selectors strictly filter to each prayer's valid range.

Adhan & Notifications

Choose from Makkah, Madinah, or Alafasi adhans. Granular per-prayer controls for Adhan, pre-reminders, and Jamaat popups.

Sunnah Fasting Tracker

Auto-detects Monday/Thursday, Ayyam al-Bidh, and special Islamic dates with timely Suhur/Iftar alerts.

Global Localization

Dynamic language switching with localized Adhan Dua. Custom Fajr/Isha angles and high latitude rules for every region.
