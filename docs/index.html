Capabilities

Engineered with purpose.

Every feature is designed around the rhythm of Islamic daily life — from Fajr adhan to Tahajjud reminder, right on your Windows desktop.

Precision Prayer Tracking

Core

A real-time countdown to the next prayer with a bi-directional synchronization engine. Precision progress logic ensures daily completion reaches 100% accurately.

5 prayerstracked daily

Windows 11 Taskbar

Native

Deep OS integration with a premium Glassmorphism UI overhaul. TrafficMonitor-style taskbar bar, floating popup, and system tray icon that feel native to modern design.

Win 10 & 11supported

Adhan & Dua Popup

Spiritual

Full-screen Adhan popup at prayer time displaying the complete Dua in Arabic, transliteration, and English translation. Choose from Makkah, Madinah, or Alafasi adhans with Fajr-specific audio.

3 adhansMakkah · Madinah · Alafasi

Jamaat Reminders

Community

Set fixed congregation times for your local Masjid. A visual popup countdown timer appears before Jamaat — configurable per prayer with minutes-before alert and test popup support.

5 prayersindividual Jamaat times

Sunnah Fasting & Nafal

Fiqh-aware

Auto-detects Sunnah fasting days (Mon/Thu, Ayyam al-Bidh, Shawwal). Full Nafal schedule — Ishraq, Awwabin, Tahajjud — with Prohibited prayer times (Sunrise, Zawal, Sunset). Ramadan mode with live Suhur/Iftar countdown.

Ramadanmode included

Calculation & Localization

Global

Multiple calculation methods (Karachi, ISNA, MWL & more), Hanafi/Shafi'i Asr, Hijri date adjustment, latitude rules for high-latitude regions. Interface in English & Bangla with custom gradient colors and layout modes.

v2.0.0latest release

Daily Deed & Amal Tracking

New

Integrated spiritual deed logging system. Track your daily good deeds (Amal) alongside prayers with an intuitive tabbed interface for a complete spiritual overview.
